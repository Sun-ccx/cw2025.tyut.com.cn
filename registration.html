Registration

(All registration must be completed by 5 September 2025)
REGISTRATION FEE:
Category Registration Type Fee Note
Regular Full Registration for Full and Short Papers 3800 CNY (520 USD) Each full registration covers only one accepted paper, regardless of paper type.
Second Full/Short Paper Registration (Same Author) 3040 CNY (416 USD) For the same author(s), the first paper requires full registration; the second paper receives a 20% discount.
Third or Additional Full/Short Papers (Same Author) 2660 CNY (364 USD) For the same author(s), the first paper requires full registration; the second paper receives a 20% discount, and the third or subsequent papers receive a 30% discount.
Poster Papers 3040 CNY (416 USD) ---
Extra Accompanying Person Fee 3040 CNY (416 USD) Applicable if she/he has one or more accepted papers.
Participant Listener Fee 3040 CNY (416 USD) Applicable to participants without any accepted papers.

Note

All registration types include: access to all technical sessions, a conference bag, the final program, buffet lunches and dinners, the conference banquet, and refreshment breaks throughout the event.

Payment Instructions:

（1）Regular Paper Registration: When making your payment, please include a note with your paper ID and surname.
For example, if your paper ID is 7670 and the presenter (author)'s name is Jianfeng Wang, the note should be: "7670+Wang".
(如果您的论文是Regular Paper，当您缴费付款时，请添加备注，格式是"论文编号+姓"，例如某篇论文的编号是7670，作报告的作者姓名是Jianfeng Wang，那么这篇论文缴费注册时需要备"7670+Wang")

（2）Extra Registration: If you are registering as an Extra, please include a note when making your payment in the format "E+paper ID+Name".
For example, if your paper ID is 7670 and your name is Jianfeng Wang, the note should be "E+7670+Jianfeng Wang".
(如果您的论文是Regular Paper，当您缴费付款时，请添加备注，格式是"论文编号+姓"，例如某篇论文的编号是7670，作报告的作者姓名是Jianfeng Wang，那么这篇论文缴费注册时需要备"7670+Wang)

（3）Participant Registration: If you are registering as a Participant, please include a note when making your payment in the format "P+Name".
For example, if your name is Jianfeng Wang, the note should be "P+Jianfeng Wang".
(如果您是Participant的注册类型，当您缴费付款时，请添加备注，格式是"P+姓名"，例如Jianfeng Wang，那么缴费注册时需要备注"P+Jianfeng Wang")
