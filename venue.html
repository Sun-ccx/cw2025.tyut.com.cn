Taiyuan University of Technology

Address: No.209, University Street, Yuci District, Jinzhong City

Taiyuan University of Technology is a century-old institution with a long history,profound heritage,and distinctive characteristics.It is located in Taiyuan,a national historical and cultural city with a history of over 2,500 years.

The university focuses primarily on engineering while integrating science and engineering with the coordinated development of multiple disciplines.It covers nine categories,including science,engineering,economics,law,education,literature,management,art,and interdisciplinary studies,and has 24 professional colleges.

Taiyuan University of Technology places great emphasis on the construction of sports culture and is the only university in China that has won national championships in both men's basketball and football.

Throughout its long history,Taiyuan University of Technology has always adhered to the motto of"Seeking Truth and Innovation"and is committed to cultivating well-rounded socialist builders and successors who develop morally,intellectually,physically,aesthetically,and laboriously.

Transportation Guide

From Taiyuan Wusu Airport

  • From Taiyuan Wusu Airport, you can take a taxi directly to Taiyuan University of Technology (Mingxiang Campus).
  • Or take the Airport Shuttle Line 1 to Wuyi Square, then transfer to Bus No. 902 to Mingxiang Campus.
  • 1.5 to 2 hours

From Taiyuan Railway Station

  • Take Bus No. 902 directly from Taiyuan Railway Station to Taiyuan University of Technology Mingxiang Campus.
  • Or take a taxi to the university
  • About 1 hour 30 minutes.

From Jinzhong Railway Station

  • Walk to the nearby bus stop at Jinzhong Railway Station
  • Then take Jinzhong Bus No.202 directly to the East Gate of Taiyuan University of Technology.
  • About 40 minutes to 1 hour.

Local Attractions

Jinci Temple
A royal sacrificial garden in Taiyuan,Shanxi,with a long history and exquisite architecture.

Wutai Mountain
A Buddhist holy land in Xinzhou,Shanxi,one of China's Four Sacred Mountains,known for its temples and scenic beauty.

Qiao Family Compound
A cluster of ancient buildings from the Ming and Qing dynasties in Qixian,Shanxi,showcasing the essence of Jin merchant culture.
